Project Name: FitBuddy - AI Fitness Plan Generator

Description:
FitBuddy is a comprehensive FastAPI web application that uses Google Gemini AI to act as a virtual personal trainer. It dynamicly generates personalized 7-day workout plans and goal-based nutrition or recovery tips. By gathering user metrics (age, weight, fitness goals, and preferred intensity), the app creates bespoke plans stored persistently in SQLite. A standout feature is the interactive feedback loop where users can submit natural language feedback to have the Gemini AI instantly refine and update their workout plan.
